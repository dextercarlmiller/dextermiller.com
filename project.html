Game AI Showcase

Two classic strategy games — both powered by the MinMax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning. Click a cell to play, or hit Computer Turn to watch the AI move.

MinMax + Alpha-Beta

Tic-Tac-Toe

The classic 3×3 game — first to get three in a row wins. Play as X against a friend, or challenge the AI as O.

Click any open square to place your mark, or press Computer Turn to let the AI make a move. The AI plays as the current player's turn. Press Replay to start a new game at any time.

Algorithm: The computer uses MinMax with alpha-beta pruning — a recursive search that explores every possible future game state, pruning branches that can't improve the outcome. It always plays optimally.
Player Turn: X
Alpha-Beta Depth 6

Connect Four

Drop pieces into the 6×7 grid. First player to connect four in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins. Yellow always goes first.

Click any cell in a column to drop your piece, or press Computer Turn to let the AI decide. The AI analyzes board position, center control, and opponent threats before every move.

Algorithm: Alpha-beta pruning at depth 6. The AI scores every 4-cell window on the board — rewarding three-in-a-row opportunities and penalizing opponent threats. Center column pieces are weighted higher, matching optimal Connect Four strategy.
Player Turn: Yellow
