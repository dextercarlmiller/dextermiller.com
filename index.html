About Me

Bridging Technology & Purpose

My journey in technology began with a deep fascination for algorithms — specifically implementing the MinMax algorithm to power game AI as part of my senior college project. That curiosity has grown into leading teams and managing complex IT infrastructures as an IT Manager.

I believe technology should serve people and strengthen connections, not replace them. Every system I build or manage is guided by that principle. My work is grounded in three core values: faith, family, and community.

Outside of work, you'll find me playing guitar, competing in pickleball and disc golf, or spending time outdoors. Life is richer when you stay curious and keep growing.
